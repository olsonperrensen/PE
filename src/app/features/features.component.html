Buy tickets

Travel through Vietnam with the use of our application. We offer you the possibility to explore this wonderful country with the ease of a train ticket! How does it work? Buy a one-way ticket and enter the place you are going to as the destination station. Note: always take a printed version of your e-ticket with you.

Create an account

With an account, you can easily:

  • Manage your account
    By going to 'My details' , you can edit your personal details and subscribe to or unsubscribe from the NS newsletter.

  • Product status
    By going to 'My products', you can view your NS products and check the remaining time on your season ticket or the status of your Keuzedagen.

  • Order status
    For a full summary of your NS orders and their statuses, go to 'My Orders'.

  • View travel history
    You can find a list of all your trips and the corresponding costs over the past 18 months by going to 'My travel history' . You can also add notes or print a summary to add to your expense claim.

  • View invoices
    If you have a season ticket, you can go to 'My invoices' to view your monthly invoices. If you have a Traject Vrij season ticket, you'll also find invoices for trips made outside your chosen route, as well as your monthly payments for your chosen route.

Locate nearest train

By granting us your location permission, we can help you find the nearest station based on your GPS coordinates. This step is only possible thanks to thanks to the Google API services.
